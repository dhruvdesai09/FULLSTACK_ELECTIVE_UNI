About Us

Since 2010, The Modern Frame has presented innovative contemporary art in immersive spaces. We partner with both new and acclaimed artists to support bold perspectives and create dialogue within the art community. Our programs include curated exhibitions, open-studio sessions, and international artist residencies.

Our Vision

“Art is not just what we see — it’s how we see. The Modern Frame exists to sharpen and expand that vision.”
